<p>I slipped off an edge into a dark void and felt the usual surge of adrenaline. Normally this is the moment when I wake up, but this time the thought flashed through my mind that I had fallen like this many times before. In the same moment I recognized that I was dreaming.</p>

<p>Because of that realization the fear vanished almost immediately, and it somehow interrupted the usual sequence. Instead of waking up at the moment of death, the falling stopped and I found myself floating in a completely black void.</p>

<p>I couldn’t stay in this state for long. I only had a short time to wonder how it was possible that I couldn’t feel my body at all. I couldn't see, hear or feel anything, only my thoughts remained.</p>

<p>After a short while my senses began to return slowly. First I faintly felt the pressure of the bed against my back. The sensation was vague at first but gradually became stronger. I tried to reverse it and go back to the void to experience it a bit longer, but then I found myself fully awake in my bed.</p>
